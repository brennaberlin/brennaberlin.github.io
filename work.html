My work

Cigna

Design Planning Workshop | Fall 2015

Our mission

The American health insurance industry is complex, proving to be confusing to a wide range of its users. Cigna prompted us to consider how their user may be better served, primarily by considering how they could shift their service to a whole healthcare experience and lifestyle-centric model, away from the existing fee for service model.

My role

Contextual research, stakeholder mapping, user research, analysis, synthesis, concept framework, and concept development.

Key takeaways

Insights framework, design principles, thinking outside the five points of interaction, concepts, and implementation roadmap.
